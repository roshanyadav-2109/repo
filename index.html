The Big Question: "Can I Really Do This Alone?"

This is the #1 concern for serious students. The answer isn't a simple 'yes' or 'no'. It's about understanding the real challenges and choosing the right strategy for **YOU**.

The Evolving Challenge: The Bar is Getting Higher

The program of today isn't the same as it was in 2021. IIT Madras is actively increasing rigor to find students who don't just memorize, but truly understand.

Harder Courses, Stricter Passing

The May 2024 term showed a significant jump in difficulty for key courses like Maths 2 and Stats 2. Even foundational subjects like Python are becoming more rigorous.

Stricter Examination Policies

With enhanced proctoring and stricter evaluation policies, there is zero room for error. The focus is entirely on academic integrity and genuine knowledge.

College is About Exploring, Not Mugging

IITM wants students who participate, build projects, and explore concepts deeply. When you're struggling alone, you're forced to mug just to survive. You lose the chance to truly learn.

The Opportunity Paradox

The IITM brand opens doors, but it's your portfolio that gets you through them. The degree alone is not enough.

Companies Hire Skills, Not Just Degrees

Recruiters will ask, "Show me your projects." A degree gets the interview; a strong portfolio of practical work gets the job.

The Portfolio Challenge

Building a job-winning portfolio while juggling rigorous courses is a major hurdle for standalone students.
